About Us

The Heating the Earth organization started with 3 boy which atended the same college in Cape Town CBD had found it consurning how much people mainly kids on how little access the had to food and how they would be hungry and continuesly asking for money or just warm foods. The 3 boys desided to take action and create a small group unnamed at the time to collect leftover foods and provide these foods to the people of there communities which had been struggling to gain access to foods ecspeically warm/hot foods.As time has gone the group would grow in size and take the name Heating the Earth, Heating being a pun on hot and eating which the group had focus on when providing foods to people in need.
