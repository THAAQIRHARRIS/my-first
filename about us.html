The FEED THE FUTURE organization started with three boys who attended the same college in Cape Town CBD. They found it concerning how many people, mainly kids, had little access to food and how they would often ask for money or warm meals. The boys decided to take action and created a small group to collect leftover foods and provide these meals to their communities. As time passed, the group grew and adopted the name FEED THE FUTURE.
