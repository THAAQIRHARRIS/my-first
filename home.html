Us at FEED THE FUTURE have an objective of providing HOT and well balanced meats to the people stuck in poverty in the Cape Town communities in order to decrease the amount of people which themselves starving and have no access to foods. To achieve our target at FEED THE FUTURE we would like to ask for donations in the form of tyoes of foods or donations in the form of money. By doing this it helps us provide suitable foods to people in dier need.

Objectives of FEED THE FUTURE include: Protecting children from all forms of malnutrition. Improving school attendance and performance. Strengthening local food systems. Ensuring healthy, well-nourished, and educated children can thrive1. Increasing food availability, accessibility, resilience, and economic participation in sustainable food ecosystems for vulnerable households

Benefits of FEED THE FUTURE include: Enhancing children's active learning capacity Alleviating short-term hunger Providing an incentive for children to attend school regularly and punctually Addressing certain micro-nutrient deficiencies Saving money for governments by using locally grown nutritious food
